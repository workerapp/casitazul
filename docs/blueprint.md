# **App Name**: La Casita Azul Landing Page

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Clear CTAs: Prominent display of the clinic's name, a warm and professional visual, and clear calls to action for scheduling appointments or viewing services.
- Comprehensive Services Showcase: Detailed presentation of veterinary services using a card-based layout with representative icons for easy understanding.
- About Us Section: Compelling narrative of the clinic's mission and values, paired with an image of the veterinary team or clinic's facade to build trust.
- Client Testimonials Carousel: Rotating display of client testimonials to provide social proof and reassurance of the clinic's quality of care.
- Interactive Contact Form and Information: Contact form for inquiries, along with direct contact information (phone, WhatsApp, address) and an embedded Google Maps for easy access and navigation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Medium sky blue (#87CEEB) to evoke a friendly and calming atmosphere.
- Secondary color: White (#FFFFFF) for a clean and spacious feel.
- Accent colors: Darker blue (#367FA9) for text and buttons; Pale Yellow (#FFFACD) for call-to-action buttons.
- Font: 'Montserrat' (sans-serif) for clear and easy readability of both headings and body text.
- Use custom, friendly icons related to veterinary services in the services section.
- Clean and professional layout with a clear hierarchy of information.
